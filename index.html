My Pivotal WIP

A simple little Sinatra app. After configuring your Pivotal Tracker name, token, and WIP limit you can visualize whether you've started too many stories at one time. Also included in /bin is a cli app to get your feet wet using the Pivotal Tracker api.

Libs:

  • http://www.sinatrarb.com
  • https://github.com/jsmestad/pivotal-tracker
  • http://jquery.com/
  • https://github.com/JEG2/highline (cli app found in /bin)
